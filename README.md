# FEAR FLASH

**FEAR FLASH** is a psychological horror game where you play as a photographer confronting your deepest fears and darkest memories. To survive, you must navigate a haunted house, capture crucial items with your camera, and evade a malevolent spirit that stalks your every move.  

With each level, you delve deeper into your past, uncovering the truth behind your partner's strange behavior and the terrifying events that led to your current predicament. Can you overcome your fears and escape before it's too late? Or will you succumb to the horrors that await?
